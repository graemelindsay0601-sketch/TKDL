# TKDL – Tesco Kitchen Darts League

## Product Overview & Investment Summary

### Executive Summary

TKDL (Tesco Kitchen Darts League) is a fully digital, gamified workplace darts competition platform designed to transform casual recreational darts into an engaging, persistent league ecosystem.

What began as a simple workplace darts competition has evolved into a complete league management, statistics, achievements, player progression, and historical records platform with the potential to serve workplaces, pubs, clubs, universities, community centres, and amateur darts leagues worldwide.

The platform combines league management software, player statistics tracking, gamification systems, achievement hunting, historical records, season archives, player profiles, rankings, and live broadcasting into a single web-based experience.

---

# The Problem

Most amateur darts leagues suffer from several issues:

* Results are recorded manually.
* Historical statistics are lost between seasons.
* Players have little incentive beyond winning individual games.
* Casual players struggle to stay engaged.
* No meaningful progression system exists.
* League administration becomes increasingly time-consuming as participation grows.

Traditional spreadsheets solve record keeping but fail to create long-term engagement.

TKDL was designed to solve this problem.

---

# The Solution

TKDL turns every match into part of a larger player journey.

Players do not simply play darts.

They:

* Earn points
* Climb rankings
* Unlock achievements
* Build career records
* Collect season titles
* Improve ratings
* Build legacies

The platform automatically tracks and presents all of this through a modern web experience.

# Core Platform Features

## Live League Leaderboard

Real-time ranking system showing:

* Current position
* Movement up/down rankings
* Wins
* Losses
* Games played
* Elo rating
* Tier ranking
* Current points

The leaderboard updates dynamically as matches are submitted.

---

## Monthly Seasons

TKDL operates using seasonal competition structures.

Each season:

* Starts with equal player balances
* Runs independently
* Produces a champion
* Creates historical records
* Archives standings permanently

This allows infinite replayability without losing historical achievements.

---

## Career Statistics Engine

Beyond seasonal performance, every player accumulates career statistics:

* Career wins
* Career losses
* Career games played
* Career win percentage
* Longest win streaks
* Historical achievements
* Lifetime rankings

This creates long-term investment for players.

---

## Player Profiles

Every player receives a fully interactive profile page.

Features include:

* Career statistics
* Seasonal performance
* Win rates
* Current ranking
* Historical achievements
* Seasonal history
* Streak tracking
* Achievement showcases

The profile system effectively acts as a personal sporting resume.

---

## Achievement System

One of TKDL's strongest differentiators.

Players unlock achievements for:

* Match wins
* Win streaks
* High point balances
* Seasonal milestones
* League participation
* Special accomplishments

Achievements create additional goals beyond simply winning games.

This dramatically increases engagement among lower-ranked players.

---

## Elo Rating System

A competitive skill rating system calculates player performance independently of seasonal points.

Benefits:

* Tracks skill growth over time.
* Creates fairer player comparisons.
* Allows long-term ranking systems.
* Adds prestige beyond seasonal results.

---

## Historical Season Archive

Every completed season becomes permanently accessible.

Users can review:

* Final standings
* Historical champions
* Previous rankings
* Archived records

The league effectively builds its own history over time.

---

## Live Match Submission

Players submit results through a web interface.

The platform automatically:

* Validates players
* Records results
* Updates standings
* Updates statistics
* Updates achievements
* Updates rankings

No manual spreadsheet maintenance is required.

---

## Broadcasting & Display Mode

A dedicated broadcast interface provides:

* Large-screen displays
* Live standings
* Featured player cards
* Match updates
* Event presentation

Designed for workplace TVs, tournaments, and public display screens.
